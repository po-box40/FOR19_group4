About us

Our team, consisting of 7 developers, students at NHH - Norwegian School of Economics, has a shared passion for economics and IT. This semester, under the guidance of our teacher Mario Blázquez de Paz, we took the class of Green Digitalization and App Development, learning languages like HTML, CSS, Flask, Latex, Python, and JavaScript.

Our app, Greenify, aims to make a positive impact on the environment by promoting sustainable practices. We believe in the power of technology to drive positive change. Our diverse team, with members from different backgrounds, brings unique perspectives to creatively address complex issues. Connect with us on Linkedin or visit our website to meet the team and learn more about our commitment to building a sustainable future.
